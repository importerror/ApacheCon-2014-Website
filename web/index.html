About

Join us at the first edition of the Apache India Mini Conference, taking place on May 6, 2013 at the beautiful campus of InMobi and be a part of the history. Apache India Mini Conference is a premier Apache Software Foundation (ASF) event in Bangalore that features multiple sessions on Apache top-level projects. Designed around the theme Cloud and Data Handling, this half-day Open Source gathering presents an excellent opportunity to connect with a unique blend of Apache community members, developers, users, and industry leaders. Presenters include renowned members from the Apache community, as well as representatives from InMobi, CloudByte, and Citrix. If you're interested in Apache CloudStack, Apache Hadoop, GSOC, The Apache Way, or any of the other top-level Apache project, this is the opportunity to learn, share your knowledge, and hit upon new ideas. Through highly relevant sessions, you will gain first-hand insight on top-notch technologies developed under the Apache umbrella. This event is open for everyone! Anyone interested in Apache products is welcome: ASF affiliation is not required to present at, attend, or otherwise participate in Apache India Mini Conference.
